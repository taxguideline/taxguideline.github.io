Install TurboTax Using My Product Key

Installing TurboTax using your product key is a simple process that involves downloading or inserting the CD, entering your product key during installation, and signing into your Intuit account.
 

 

Use  Installturbotax.com and receive the benefits of using a user-friendly interface also you need to select the appropriate version of TurboTax & review additional features during purchases of software.

 

How do I download TurboTax?

Depending on how you obtained TurboTax, follow one of these steps:

  1. Go to Installturbotax.com in your web browser.
  2. Select the version of TurboTax you purchased (e.g., Deluxe, Premier).
  3. For Download: Click on the download link for your operating system (Windows or macOS). The file will typically be saved in your downloads folder.
  4. Accept License Agreement.
  5. During the installation, you need to find out the product key.
  6. Choose a destination folder for the installation (or leave it as default).
  7. Click Install and wait for the process to complete.
  8. If prompted, enter the 16-character license code from your purchase confirmation email or CD packaging.
  9. Begin entering your tax information and follow the on-screen guidance.

How do I accept a TurboTax License Agreement?

Accept License Agreement during installation, you will be prompted to accept the TurboTax License Agreement.

  1. Open TurboTax: Start the Installturbotax.com on your computer or go to the TurboTax website if you're using the online version.
  2. Agree to the Terms: During the installation process or when you first sign in (depending on the version), you will be prompted to read the License Agreement.
  3. Read the Agreement: You’ll likely be presented with the full License Agreement or a link to view it. Take a moment to read through the terms, though you may be able to skip through it if you choose.
  4. Accept: Read through it and click "Accept" to proceed.
  5. Continue: Once you’ve accepted, you’ll be able to continue using TurboTax.

If you’re using TurboTax online, this might happen when you first log in or when you’re about to file your taxes.
